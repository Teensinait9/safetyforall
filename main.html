ABOUT US

"Meirim" is a sustainable development project aimed at fostering a culture of waste sorting and recycling.

Each of us can contribute to the protection of the environment by handing in at least one kilogram of waste paper and unnecessary things to a collection point or organizing waste sorting on the scale of our apartment.

This project contributes to fostering a responsible attitude towards the environment among citizens. Our task is to make our world environmentally friendly and preserve its beauty for future generations!
